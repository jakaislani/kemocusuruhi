Bob Kelly is an anchor on Fox News 29 who is rumored to be leaving the network. However, he has confirmed that he is not going anywhere right now. 

In October 2014, Bob Kelly decided to join the FOX 29 News team as a traffic reporter for Good Day Philadelphia.

He also presents Kelly’s Classroom, Breakfast with Bob, and Town Takeover, which all take him out into the community to meet with Good Day Philadelphia viewers.

He has also anchored the Philadelphia St. Patrick’s Day Parade for over 15 years. Bob spent 13 years as the Traffic Anchor at CBS 3 in Philadelphia before joining FOX 29.

He was the first traffic anchor in Philadelphia to perform his reports in front of a green screen. Bob is now known as The Dean of Traffic because of his animated personality and iconic expressions like Jammo, Two Jelly Donut Delay, and the Conshy Curve.
