Virender Raj Anand, popularly known as Tinnu Anand, who is a veteran Indian actor, director and has worked in numerous Bollywood films. Mostly, he played the roles of a protagonist and known for working in Amitabh Bachchan’s movies, like; “Kaalia” which was the best release of 1981, “Main Azaad Hoon” (1989), “Major Saab”, most liked the film, released in 1998 and the list goes on. It seems Amitabh Bachchan is great admiration for Tinnu Anand.

Tinnu Anand was born on 12th October 1945, India and he is the son of the most famed writer Inder Raj Anand, who is the brother of film producer Bittu Anand and uncle of director Siddharth Anand. It seems Tinnu Anand has strong roots in the Bollywood film industry.

He got his education from Mayo College, India, and one of his interviews, Tinnu Anand praised the generosity of Amitabh Bachchan.

Career

Initially, Virender Raj Anand started his career working as an assistant director to Satyajit Ray and he worked with him for five years. He worked in Bengali films as well and some of his movies are notable, such as; “Ashani Sanket”(1973), “Seemabaddha” (1971), and others.

His first directorial as an assistant director in Bengali movie “Goopy Gyne Bagha Byne” which was released in 1969. Since then, he directed the number of Bengali movies and got famed.

Later on, he started working as an actor in Bollywood movies and appeared in a number of movies. Also, he worked as a director and writer as well and marked his identity in multiple fields.

Mostly, he worked in Amitabh Bachchan movies as he is the real inspiration for him. Tinnu Anand directed and produced the film “Shahenshah” which was the most popular release of the year 1988. In the film, Amitabh Bachchan worked alongside Meenakshi Sheshadri. While the film was written by Amitabh Bachchan’s wife Jaya Bachchan, who is also a veteran actress as well.

Since then, he worked as a director and directed and produced a large number of Bollywood films including “Kaalia”, starring Amitabh Bachchan, “Mein Azaad Hoon”, Major Saab”, (1998), “Yeh Ishq Nahin Asan”, “Dunya Meri Jeb Main” and so on.

While he made a broad career in Bollywood films as he appeared as an actor and mostly portrayed negative roles in the movies for which he earned huge recognition throughout his career.

Achievements

He earned a lot of fame, popularity, critical acclaim for his outstanding work and he calls it as the major achievement of his life.
