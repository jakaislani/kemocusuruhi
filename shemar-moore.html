An American actor and fashion model, Shemar Franklin Moore is known for his incredibly amazing contribution to the film and fashion industry. He is recognized as one of the top-notched stars whose roles include Malcolm Winters on ‘The Young and the Restless’ started in 1994 and ended in 2005.

Among others, he became famous for appearing as Derek Morgan on ‘CBS Criminal Minds’ (2005-2016), Hondo on ‘S.W.A.T’ 2017-present), both are the longest-running series in which he gave fantabulous performances.

Career

The most talented man Shemar Moore established his career in both the acting and modeling fields and has been successfully managing his career life. Besides, he is known as a host who hosted the show named ‘Soul Train’, which started in 1999 and ended in 2003.

Moore has performed in many films, short movies, television series, and web series. Also, he has worked as a fashion model and has modeled for a variety of notable fashion brands.

He is an award-winning actor who has won eight NAACP Image Awards and also won the 2000 Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series for his work on ‘The Young and the Restless’.

Moreover, Moore has earned a nomination for a People’s Choice Award in the year 2016 for his work on ‘Criminal Minds’.

Achievements

The award-winning actor is the recipient of many awards including the NAACP Image Awards as well as the Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series in 2000. Also, he has earned various nominations including the People’s Choice Award in 2016.
