Sarkodie, Lil Wayne, Stonebwoy, Shaggy, Others To Perform At Beale Street Music Festival

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, April 25, 2024

Highly rated Ghanaian recording acts, Sarkodie and Stonebwoy have been officially announced as part of the artists that will be performing at this year’s Beale Street Music Festival.

The 2022 Beale Street Music Festival which is scheduled to take place at ‘FairGround In Liberty Park’ In Memphis, Tennessee will run for three days, Friday, April 29, 2022 to Sunday, May 1, 2022.

The annual music festival will see the SarkCess Music head, Sarkodie

and BHIM Nation President, Stonebwoy perform alongside top American stars including Lil Wayne, Megan Thee Stallion, Waka Flocka, Jamaican-American reggae musician, Shaggy, among others.
