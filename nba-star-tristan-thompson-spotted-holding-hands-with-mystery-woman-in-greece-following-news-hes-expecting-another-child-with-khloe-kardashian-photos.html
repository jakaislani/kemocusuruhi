NBA Star, Tristan Thompson Spotted Holding Hands with Mystery Woman in Greece After News of Another

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, May 10, 2024

Recent sightings have shown Tristan Thompson holding hands with an unidentified woman in Greece, days after it was revealed that he and his on-again, off-again girlfriend Khloé Kardashian are expecting their second child through a surrogate.

A viral video captured the Chicago Bulls center-forward walking through busy streets with a woman sporting a skintight orange and red dress.

This comes after confirmation that the estranged couple is expecting their second child through a surrogate.

Khloé Kardashian and Tristan Thompson’s surrogate became pregnant in November 2021 amidst the athlete’s then-secret paternity lawsuit with Maralee Nichols, who later gave birth to their son, Theo, on Dec. 1.

“Khloé and Tristan already had the baby in the works when the scandal hit. By then, Khloé was just like, ‘I’m going to be doing it by myself,’” an insider told PageSix. Another source revealed that Kardashian had been openly discussing having the baby with friends since before Christmas.

Khloé and Tristan’s relationship has encountered rough patches over the years due to his infidelity scandal.
