Best Sweet Pickle Relish to Buy at the Grocery Store

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, June 5, 2024

The best sweet pickle relish is sweet but still maintains a slight pickle-y tang, making it the perfect addition to any type of creamy, mayo-based salad—tuna salad, egg salad, the list goes on.

I always identified as someone who doesn’t like sweet pickles or sweet pickle relish. For me, it was dill or nothing. Our sweet pickle relish taste test, however, really opened my eyes. There is such a spectrum of sweet pickle relishes out there. Whether you’re a diehard fan of the sweet, bread and butter pickle flavor or a dill devotee like me, there is a sweet pickle relish out there for you. If you’re looking for the best sweet pickle relish, buy one of these.

Best for Sweet Haters

Kroger Sweet Pickle RelishInstacart

To me, Kroger’s sweet pickle relish is basically dill pickle relish. It’s so tart and tangy with just a slight hint of sweetness. For me, this is ideal. It ranks low on this list simply because I understand that people will typically want more sweetness than what Kroger’s relish offers. That being said, if you’re open to mixing it up or you love zesty pickles like me, grab a jar of this relish!

Best Classic

Vlasic Kosher Sweet Pickle RelishWalmart

Vlasic Kosher Sweet Pickle Relish is exactly what I think of when I think of sweet pickle relish. It’s a little crunchy, goopy (in the best way), and perfectly sugary. This is what your mom puts in that delicious tuna salad she made for you when you were growing up. If you’re looking for that sweet, bright-green nostalgia for egg or potato salad, Vlasic’s is the best sweet pickle relish to get the job done.

Best Packaging

Heinz Sweet Pickle RelishTarget

Heinz Sweet Pickle Relish is a nice, classic sweet pickle relish in the world’s most convenient packaging. Why you’d ever put sweet pickle relish into a jar rather than this perfectly engineered squeeze bottle is beyond me. When there are so many varieties of a product out there, you have to start looking at all of the item’s aspects. Not only does this sweet pickle relish taste good, it’s quicker and easier to use than other brands. You can squirt it directly onto your hot dog! Great taste and convenient packaging make Heinz one of the best sweet pickle relish makers.

Best of the Best

Del Monte Sweet Pickle RelishRalphs

Del Monte’s sweet pickle relish is the best sweet pickle relish we tried. It converted me from a sweet relish hater to a sweet relish lover. This sweet relish is made with real sugar, too!
