Joey Logano Wiki Biography

Joseph Thomas Logano was born on the 24th May 1990 in Middletown, Connecticut USA, and is of Italian ancestry. Joey Logano, he is a stock car racer, currently competing in the NASCAR Sprint Cup Series and NASCAR Xfinity series for the team Penske, but previously for the Joe Gibbs Racing team. His career has been active since 2007.

Have you ever wondered how rich Joey Logano is? According to sources, it has been estimated that Joey`s overall net worth is $18 million, an amount acquired through his successful career as a race driver, during which he became the youngest racer to win the Nationwide Series, and the youngest Spring Cup Series winner too.

Joey Logano Net Worth $18 Million

Joey`s love for cars was first expressed when he was six years old, entering the Quarter Midget racing, and next year, he won the Eastern Grand National Championship in the Jr. Stock Car Division. He continued to line up success after success in his early years, winning Jr. Honda Division Championship and Lt. Mod. Division Championship in 1999.

When he was ten, his family moved to Georgia, and soon after arriving, Joey entered the Bandolero series, and won the Bandolero Bandits Series Championship. Joey continued to record success, taking 14 consecutive wins in the Legend cars series.

He joined Grand National NASCAR division when he was 17, as until 2007, NASCAR didn`t allow racers younger than 16 to enter the competition, and ever since then, he has been a dominant force in the championships. In his first season, he competed in 13 races and he managed to win five of them, including the Greenville-Pickens Speedway, Iowa Speedway, and two wins at New Hampshire International Speedway. Of course his net worth rose with each victory.

Joey entered the NASCAR Nationwide Sprint Cup series in 2008, making his debut on the Dover International Speedway in the 2008 Heluva Good! 200. However, his first victory came two races later, on the Meijer 300 at Kentucky Speedway. Joey began to slowly build-up his professional career, and so far he has 14 wins in the NASCAR Championship, which has became the main source of his net worth.

During his career he has won in races on the Kansas Speedway, Auto Club Speedway, Michigan International Speedway, Chicagoland Speedway, Dover International Speedway, and numerous others which has increased his net worth by a large degree.

Apart from his successful racing career, Joey has been in various TV shows; in 2009 he was a co-host of the WWE Raw episode, together with his teammate Kyle Busch, and in 2010, he was featured in an episode of Disney`s TV series “Destroy Build Destroy”, with Carl Edwards. Furthermore, he appeared as a guest star in “Lab Rats” in 2013, and also appeared in the film “Sharkanado 3: “Oh Hell No” (2015). Joey has also been featured in the “60 Minutes”, as a part of the Make A Wish Foundation, since the child, Gavin Grubbs wanted to meet him.

Regarding his personal life, he has been married to Brittany Baca since 13th December 2014; Brittany is his childhood sweetheart.
