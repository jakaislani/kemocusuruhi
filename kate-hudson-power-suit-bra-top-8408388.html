Kate Hudson Reworked the Classic Power Suit With a Bandeau Bra

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 16, 2024

If you have yet to step into your suit era, now’s your chance. Regardless of the season, the classic power suit is a strong choice with endless possibilities to add a touch of feminine flair. Take, for instance, Selena Gomez's Barbiecore version or Jennifer Lopez, whose skirt suit included a crop top vest. And, now, Kate Hudson is test-driving the trend with her own sexy take on the three-piece suit.

On Wednesday, the actress stepped out for the 2023 WIF Max Mara Face of the Future Award Recipient cocktail event in Los Angeles while putting a sultry twist on menswear. For the evening, she wore an oversized dark gray blazer with a pair of matching wide-leg pleated trousers and nothing but an itty-bitty black bandeau top underneath. She matched her nails to the dark hue of her suit and accessorized with a pair of diamond drop earrings, black stilettos, and a coordinating clutch.

Her beauty look included tousled blonde waves parted down the middle, a glowing complexion, feathered lashes, and pink glossy lips.

Hudson's wardrobe will never go out of style — and earlier this month, the actress traded her heels for the most practical boot for winter: zip-front combat boots. She teamed the black booties with a pair of bright-blue cropped trousers, an oversized gray turtleneck, and a long houndstooth coat for the ultimate cozy luxe 'fit.
