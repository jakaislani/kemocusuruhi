Linda Cardellini is an American actress who has starred in various television shows and movies, ranging from comedy to drama to horror.

She is best known for her roles as Lindsay Weir on Freaks and Geeks, Samantha Taggart on ER, Judy Hale on Dead to Me, and Laura Barton on Avengers.

Linda Cardellini Ethnicity

Linda Cardellini has a diverse ethnic background.

She is of Italian, Irish, German, English, and Scottish descent

Her paternal grandfather was from Sicily, Italy, while her maternal grandparents were from Ireland and England

She was raised Catholic and attended a Catholic high school.

She has said that she is proud of her heritage and that it has influenced her as an actress.

Linda Cardellini Avengers

Linda Cardellini made her debut in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) as Laura Barton, the wife of Clint Barton/Hawkeye (Jeremy Renner), in Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015).

She reprised her role in Avengers: Endgame (2019), where she was reunited with her husband and children after they were snapped back to existence by Hulk (Mark Ruffalo).

She also appeared in the Disney+ miniseries Hawkeye (2021), which focused on the adventures of Clint and his protégé Kate Bishop (Hailee Steinfeld).

Additionally, she voiced Lylla, a sentient otter and love interest of Rocket Raccoon (Bradley Cooper), in Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 (2023).

This made her one of the few actresses to play two different characters in the MCU

Linda Cardellini Husband

Linda Cardellini has been in a long-term relationship with Steven Rodriguez, a makeup artist and sculptor.

They met when they were 10 years old and reconnected as adults.

The two have been together since 2009 and have a daughter named Lilah-Rose, who was born in 2012.

They are not married yet, but they got engaged in 2013.
