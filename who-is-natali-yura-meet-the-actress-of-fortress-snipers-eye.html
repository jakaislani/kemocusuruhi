Fortress: Sniper’s Eye- Who Is Natali Yura? Wikipedia Bio

Natali Yura is a Russian actress and singer who rose to prominence with her first single, “Yung Luv.”

She was always interested in the arts, especially music, and aspired to be a singer.

So, the performer enrolled in Sage College in Albany, New York, when she was 17 years old. During that period, she began to perform as a vocalist and met Martin Bregman, who assisted her in gaining admission to the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute.

The actress made her acting debut in 2017 with the horror/action film “Dead Trigger” and also played Maribel in the action/thriller film “Reprisal,” starring Bruce Willis.

As of now, she does not have a Wikipedia bio on the web, although she might have one anytime soon. Instead, there is a short bio with all her work credentials mentioned on IMDb.

Originally, she was born in South Korea. Along with being an actor, Natali is also a producer.

How Old Is Natali Yura Age?

Natali Yura was born on April 4, 1991. She is currently 31 years old. She was born and raised in Vladivostok, in the household of businessman and politician Yuri Stepanchenko.

Slowly, she rose to prominence as Celeste in the thriller film “The Row,” which was released on July 27, 2018.

The actress has always had a passion for the arts. According to her IMDb page, she has appeared in ten productions as an actress, which is a tremendous accomplishment for such a talented performer.

Besides all the movies she has been a part of, Yung Luv is a short video in which the actress has been a producer.
