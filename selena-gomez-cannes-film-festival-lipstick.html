Selena Gomez Takes on the Cannes Film Festival in Glamorous Style, Thanks to This $20 Lipstick

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 15, 2024

Selena Gomez is in rare form at the 2024 Cannes Film Festival. The actress pulled out three beyond stunning looks with understated but flawless detail — think ‘princess’ worthy dresses and accessories, similar to Princess Diana’s signature style. Gomez first greeted fans in a white knit peplum dress by Self Portrait, with a zig-zagged patterned white mid-size purse, white and black clasped kitten heels, and retro-style sunglasses.

The Rare Beauty founder then posted her next look on Instagram — giving royalty, chic, and timeless in every way. Gomez sported an off-the-shoulder Saint Laurent gown in black with white detailing at the top. She then accessorized with the Bvlgari Diamond Swan necklace (which took 2000 hours to create) and Santoni heels. But what really took the dress to the next level — aside from the estimated $3.5 million necklace — is the effortless makeup. Gomez went for a matte look that blends into the skin like a dream. And to no surprise, it’s all thanks to Rare Beauty.

The Only Murders In the Building star wore the $20 Kind Words Matte Lipstick in shade talented (true neutral beige) paired with the matching $15 Kind Words lipliner. The singer’s makeup artist for the festival, Hung Vanngo, posted a full product breakdown, including the Rare Beauty Perfect Strokes Universal Volumizing Mascara and one of the brand’s newest launches — the Soft Pinch Luminous Powder Blush in shade joy. Gomez’s last look was a custom, vibrant red Giambattista Valli Haute Couture dress with same-colored heels.

Gomez was present at the Cannes Film Festival to attend a photocall of her new film, Emilia Pérez, which received a nine-minute standing ovation from the crowd. In sequence with exciting projects, the Love On singer has also been creating internet buzz with the production of the upcoming Wizards of Waverly Place sequel television series, named Wizards Beyond Waverly Place.
