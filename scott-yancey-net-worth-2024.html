Scott Yancey’s Early Life and Career

Scott Yancey was born on July 9, 1969, in Los Angeles, California. He developed an interest in real estate at a young age, purchasing his first property at the age of 14. He later moved to Las Vegas, where he started his real estate investment company, Goliath Company.
