Get Familiar With Johnny Bananas Devenanzio Dating History: Who is He Dating Now?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, April 25, 2024

Johnny ‘Bananas’ Devenanzio is the heart and soul of the American reality television series The Challenge. He is one of the most accomplished reality stars in America, with an impeccable professional record.

So, today, we are going to take a look inside Johnny ‘Bananas’ Devenanzio dating history. Likewise, we will find out if he currently has a girlfriend or not. But one thing is sure: the macho man is quite famous among ladies because of his wit and good looks.

Some Facts About the American Reality Television Star

  • Devenanzio was born in Fullerton, California, on June 22, 1982. He is famous for his nickname, Johnny Bananas, which he got during his college days.
  • The American native’s full name is John Amadeus Devenanzio. His parents are Regis Devenanzio and Laura Avery. He rarely talks about his parents, but they must be proud of his achievements.
  • Johnny is the most distinguished and notorious participant in The Challenge. Also, he is the most successful Challenge winner, with seven seasons to his credit as of 2023.
  • He is renowned for several notorious incidents from The Challenge, including retaining the $275,000 that Sarah Rice, the winning pair, earned in Rivals 3.
  • With $1,184,720 in prize money, he is the second-highest prize earner among all competitors. For the record, Chris Tamburello is also one of the highest earnings contestants on The Challenge.

Discussing Johnny ‘Bananas’ Devenanzio Dating History: Who Has He Dated?

The American reality star is a charmer and a sweet talker. Therefore, it would not be an exaggeration to say that he has been linked with multiple beautiful women outside his profession.

First and foremost, Devenanzio began a romantic relationship with Olympic champion Hannah Teter in 2012. The relationship lasted for five years before calling it quits in 2017. He did not reveal the reason behind their breakup.

Similarly, The Challenge legend was in a relationship with Big Brother: Over the Top Winner Morgan Willet until 2021. The lovely couple met in 2018 on the thirty-third season of Challenge and quickly became friends.

After Morgan was eliminated from the show, she accompanied Johnny on his travel and created a buzz about their possible relationship. Their chemistry during their travel was proof of their relationship.

Further, they celebrated their one-year anniversary in 2019, but it was not until 2020 that the Challenge legend shared his first photo with his then-girlfriend. Despite their lovely relationship, the couple called it quits in 2021.

Willet later revealed that Johnny cheated on her, and that was the reason for their breakup. Maybe the reality star didn’t want to commit, or he might be afraid of the wedding.

Dating Rumors With Other Cast Members

Being a vast reality television star, Johnny ‘Bananas’ Devenanzio dating history is not complete without some rumors and hoaxes.

Johnny appeared alongside Moriah Tease on The Challenge: Ride or Dies in 2022. Tease and Devenanzio quickly became acquainted with each other, and fans of the show began to speculate about their relationship.

After the show, Moriah revealed that she was still in contact with Bananas and even wanted to get to know him more. But, when a fan asked her about her relationship status, she said that she was single.

This statement proved that even though the cast mates showed a spark in their chemistry, they did not become a couple.

Similarly, the American national was also linked with House of Villans contestant Corinne Olympios, but once again, the news turned out to be fake.
