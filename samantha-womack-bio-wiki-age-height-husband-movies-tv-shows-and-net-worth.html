Samantha Womack Biography

Samantha Womack is an English actress, singer, model as well as a director who has worked in film, television, and stage. She was born and raised in Brighton, the United Kingdom as Samantha Zoe Womack.

She made her name in the mid-1990s in her role as Mandy Wilkins in Game On leading roles in the BBC One drama Pie in the Sky and also the sitcom Babes in the Wood.
