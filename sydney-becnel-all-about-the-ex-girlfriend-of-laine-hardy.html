When did Sydney and Laine first meet?

Laine and Sydney have been dating since July of last year. Laine and Sydney’s Lives

Sydney has always been there for Laine during his time on American Idol. She even relocated from Louisiana to Los Angeles to support Laine in the final season of American Idol. Sydney, on the other hand, posted a photo with another guy on social media in July 2019.
