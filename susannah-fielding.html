Susannah Fielding is a British actress who won Ian Charleson Award for her role as Portia in The Merchant of Venice at the Almeida Theatre. She was born in Poole, England and spent her early years in Havant, near Portsmouth.

Fielding appeared in episodes of amongst most famous series like Wallander, The Bill, Doctor Who, and Black Mirror. She achieved widespread recognition for her role in The Great Indoors and later appeared with Steve Coogan in This Time with Alan Partridge.

Fielding has dated her fellow actor Tom Hiddleston during their time filming Wallander.
